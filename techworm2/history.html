A Brief History of Computer Viruses

Viruses have evolved from experiments to global threats.

  1. Creeper (1971): The very first experiment.
  2. Brain (1986): The first PC virus.
  3. ILOVEYOU (2000): A massive email worm.
  4. Stuxnet (2010): Targeted industrial systems.
