Prevention & Protection

Simple habits that dramatically reduce your risk.

Daily Habits (Unordered)

  • Keep your operating system and apps updated.
  • Use a reputable antivirus and keep it active.
  • Don't click suspicious email attachments.
  • Use strong, unique passwords with a password manager.
  • Enable two-factor authentication.
  • Back up important data weekly.

If You Suspect Infection (Ordered Steps)

  1. Disconnect from the internet immediately.
  2. Run a full antivirus scan.
  3. Quarantine or delete infected files.
  4. Change passwords from a clean device.
  5. Restore from a known-good backup.
  6. Report the incident if data was stolen.
