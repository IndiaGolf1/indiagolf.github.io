A Brief History of Computer Viruses

From academic experiments to global cyberweapons.

YearNameSignificance
1971CreeperFirst experimental self-replicating program on ARPANET.
1986BrainFirst PC virus — infected MS-DOS boot sectors.
1999MelissaEmail-based macro virus that overwhelmed servers.
2000ILOVEYOUVBScript worm caused billions in damages.
2003SQL SlammerInfected 75,000 servers in 10 minutes.
2010StuxnetNation-state malware targeting industrial systems.
2017WannaCryGlobal ransomware exploiting EternalBlue.
2021Colonial PipelineDarkSide ransomware halted U.S. fuel supply.

Key Lessons Learned

  1. Patching software vulnerabilities is essential.
  2. User education prevents most successful attacks.
  3. Backups are the best ransomware insurance.
  4. Cybersecurity is a continuous process.
