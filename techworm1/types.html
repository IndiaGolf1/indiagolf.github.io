Common Types of Malware

Malware Type Description Primary Threat Level
Virus Attaches to legitimate files and spreads when executed. High
Worm Standalone malware that replicates to spread across networks. Severe
Trojan Disguises itself as legitimate software to trick users. High
Ransomware Encrypts user data and demands payment for decryption. Severe

Common Symptoms of an Infection

  • Unexpected pop-up windows and advertisements.
  • Unusually slow computer performance.
  • Frequent crashes or "Blue Screen of Death" (BSOD) errors.
  • Unknown programs starting automatically upon boot.
  • Mass emails being sent from your account without permission.
